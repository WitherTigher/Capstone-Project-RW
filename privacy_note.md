## Privacy Note

ReadRight processes limited data to support reading improvement features.

**Data Collected**
- Student audio recordings (only if retention is enabled by the teacher)
- Attempt metadata: timestamp, duration, word, score
- Teacher account information (authentication only)

**Purpose**
- Audio is used for teacher review of pronunciation.
- Metadata supports progress analytics such as improvement trends and most-missed words.

**Retention**
- Audio is stored only when the teacher enables per-student retention.
- If retention is off, recordings are not kept after scoring.

**Access**
- Only the authenticated teacher for the class can access recordings and analytics.
- Students cannot view stored recordings.
- No data is shared with third parties.

**Security**
- All uploads use encrypted HTTPS.
- Storage buckets prevent public access.

**Permissions**
- Microphone access is used solely for recording practice attempts.

**User Rights**
- Teachers may toggle retention at any time.
- Previously stored audio can be deleted upon request.
